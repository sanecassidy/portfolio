S. Sane Cassidy's Project Portfolio and Résumé

I am a mechanical designer and engineer, currently in search of new employment opportunities.

Over the past fourteen years, I have helped to develop products in a number of different industries, including sports equipment, industrial cases and shipping containers, medical devices, retail cosmetics, and optical transceivers for the semiconductor industry.

I think the things that define me best, however, are the personal projects that I've worked on in school or as hobbies. I have put together this simple website to demonstrate some of the things that I have enjoyed working on the most.

When not at work, I'm a doting uncle to three nephews, a niece, and a couple of "fake nieces" (the children of a good friend from college).

I'm also a fan of unusual human-powered vehicles, a casual (very casual) videogamer, and an amateur escape-room enthusiast.
